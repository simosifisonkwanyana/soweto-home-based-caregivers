ABOUT US

VISION:

to build a healthy community where everyone is taken cared of, physically, socially, emotionally and economically.

MISSION STATEMENT:

Providing and promoting comprehensive health care services to people infected by chronic diseases and disabilities as well as giving support and counseling to the affected families. to build a healthy community where everyone is taken cared of, physically, socially, emotionally and economically.

HISTORY:

Soweto Home based care was established in 2002 in response to the high demand for care workers who were assigned to take care of bedridden patients within our community. Our care workers are qualified and acquired the much needed skills to perform their tasks.
